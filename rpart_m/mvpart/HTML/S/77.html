   2 /*
   3 ** The main workhorse of the recursive partitioning module.  When called
   4 **   with a node, it partitions it and then calls itself to partition the
   5 **   children it has created.
   6 ** If the node is not splittable (too few people, or complexity is too small)
   7 **   it simply returns.  The routine may not be able to discover that the
   8 **   complexity is too small until after the children have been partitioned,
   9 **   so it needs to check this at the end.
  10 */
